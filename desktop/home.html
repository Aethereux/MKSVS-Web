Welcome to MKSVS Enterprise Corp!

Have you ever found yourself locked out of your home and don't know what to do next? Don't worry; MasterLock PH is here to save the day! We are your reliable and professional locksmith service in Metro Manila and nearby provinces.

Why a Locksmith is Required in Every Home?

At MasterLock PH, we understand the importance of security and peace of mind in your home. That's why having a trusted locksmith on hand is essential. Here's why you need us:

  • Emergency Lockout Services: Accidentally locked out of your home? Our expert locksmiths are just a call away. We respond promptly and efficiently, helping you regain access to your property without any hassle.
  • Safe-Vault Services: Keep your valuable possessions secure with our safe-vault maintenance and repair services. Our trained technicians can change combinations, reset numbers, and perform maintenance to keep your safe in optimal condition.
  • Automotive and Car Locksmith Services: Locked out of your car? Our car locksmith experts will come to your rescue anytime, anywhere within Metro Manila and nearby provinces. We provide key fitting, fabrication, and duplication, all under the confirmed supervision of the car owner for added security.
  • Residential and Commercial Locksmith Services: Whether you need key fabrication for lost keys, re-keying for added security, or master key systems, we've got you covered. We also specialize in aluminum glass door alignment, repair of defective door hinges, and much more.
  • Door Access Control System & CCTV: Upgrade your security with our advanced access control systems, including proximity card access, biometric/fingerscan access, keypad access, and time attendance systems. We also supply and install CCTV systems for enhanced safety.
