HISTORY

MKSVS ENTERPRISES OPC, formerly Masterlock key and Safe-Vault Specialist, is a leading licensed professional locksmith in the Philippines based in Makati City. The company was established in 1994 by Mr. Alain V. Guillermo, a young and energetic individual with vast experience in this field. He received training and orientation in a highest level from SECURTECH LOCKSMITH, a professional locksmith company based in Texas, USA, and MANTON SECURITY in Hong Kong, China.

We provide specific solutions and services from a wide range of requirements in Access Control System, Locksmith, and Safe-Vault Services. MKSVS ENTERPRISES OPC is your partner who ensures your total satisfaction. Whatever security you need, our company has the technology to provide solutions with our partners from the world's leading Security Companies in the USA, Israel, and China.
